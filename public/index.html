ChatCord

Description of the app
Developed By Himanshu

This App is aRealtime Chat App through which you can chat over different chat channels and get a realtime experience
It Uses Express, Nodejs & Socket.io for realtime chatting.

This app has following features:
1. User can choose any channel and enter the the chat.
2. In the channel, other active clients will be notified about your entrance.
3. You can see the channel name, and the active users on side bar.
4. All will be notified when you leave
